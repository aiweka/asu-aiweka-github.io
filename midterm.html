About Me

Hello! My name is Angela. I am currently a senior studying Computer Science. I love to draw, mostly digitally, play video games, and read. My main goal is to work in the aerospace industry as an engineer, specifically with astronautical robotics.

I am deeply passionate about astronautics and the potential for space travel. Exploring the universe and understanding what lies beyond our planet has been a lifelong dream. The idea of contributing to humanity's journey through space fuels my studies and aspirations.
